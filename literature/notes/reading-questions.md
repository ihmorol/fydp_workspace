# Reading Questions for Every Paper

1. Does it solve ODE, PDE, or both?
2. Does it use plain ANN, PINN, DeepONet, or Neural ODE?
3. Does it directly address Lorenz-1960 or a similar nonlinear coupled system?
4. What are the input/output definitions of the network?
5. How are initial or boundary conditions enforced?
6. What architecture choices are fixed, and what choices are compared?
7. What baselines are used?
8. What metrics are used?
9. What exact research gap still remains after this paper?
10. Which 1-2 points are reusable in Chapter 2 or in the proposal defense?
